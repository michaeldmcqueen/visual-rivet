Conversations

CoreConversations is the catalyst for difficult conversations about critical issues that impact our individual well-being but are of community concern.

Although we have choices in life, we seldom recognize that a choice exists as we reflexively live through conditioned responses.  Through our Videos That Matter Campaign, we are working to activate change - to educate, encourage, and engage individuals on important choices.

Teams

CoreTeams equips individuals to lead conversations and be change agents.                        

Most leadership work is done on an individual level.  We have taken the approach that individual leadership is enhanced in the context of the team.  That is why our conferences bring individuals together as a team to focus on specific objectives.
